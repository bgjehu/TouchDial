Welcome to Touch Dial

Touch Dial utilizes the new 3D Touch technology so that you could make phone call without opening your Phone app, or finding people in the favorite contact.

  1. You simply setup the Touch Dial List by opening the app.
  2. Deep pressing the Touch Dial app icon, wait for menu pop up
  3. Choose the people you want to call Quick and easy like that!

We do notice that Phone app does exactly the same thing! But limited 3 slots they offer wouldn't satisfy a social animal like you! Download our extension and you come get another 4 more slots for quick dial to your significant one, family, and friends, or maybe your horrible boss!

Note: We are sorry that we only support iPhone 6s and iPhone 6s Plus at this moment.
